About Me

A brief introduction to my background, why I want to learn coding, and what my other hobbies are.


Background

I graduated from Linfield College in 2017. Afterwards I went to Japan to teach English through the JET programme. I had studied Spanish and Japanese throughout high school and college, which helped immensely with the immersion process. I lived in a "small" town called Oita, which is a place the Japanese call the boonies, but the city has a population a little over 200,000. Their comparison is Tokyo so I suppose it's understandable. I lived in Oita for two years, after which I returned to Oregon.

I had majored in political science while in college, but decided that I didn't want to entire the political world, so I have been giving marketing a try. I currently work at a digital marketing company, which is partially the reason why I wanted to pick up coding. I have also always wanted to create my own app at some point. I occasionally run across a situation where I think an app would be useful, but the perfect or ideal app doesn't exist. It might also be fun to try a hand at creating games.
